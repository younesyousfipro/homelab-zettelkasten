# Zettelkasten

Zettelkasten is a method of note-taking based on atomic notes and explicit links between ideas.

Each note represents a single idea and connects to other notes to form a network of knowledge.

## Key principles

- atomicity (one idea per note)
- linking between notes
- writing in your own words

## Mental model

Knowledge is not stored as a hierarchy but as a network of interconnected ideas.

Understanding emerges from navigating this network.
